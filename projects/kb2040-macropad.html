KB2040 Macropad (TinyUSB HID)

2025 • Embedded • USB HID • RP2040

Overview

A compact macropad built on the KB2040 board with a multi-layer keymap, an OLED UI, and Adafruit TinyUSB for HID.

Features

  • Layer-aware encoder & potentiometer logic
  • OLED layer indicator with per-layer LED colors
  • USB HID via TinyUSB (no Keyboard.h)
